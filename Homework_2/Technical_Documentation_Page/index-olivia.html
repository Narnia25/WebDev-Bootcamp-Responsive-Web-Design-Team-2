French Press

The French Press is a classic brewing method known for its simplicity and full-bodied, rich cup of coffee. This method uses a metal mesh filter, allowing oils and sediment to pass through, resulting in a more textured mouthfeel.

The key to great French Press coffee lies in grind size, water temperature, and brewing time. Experiment with different settings to discover your ideal brew.

This method also allows you to appreciate the nuances of different coffee origins and roasts, making it a great tool for home brewers.

The French Press, or cafetière, was patented by Italian designer Attilio Calimani in 1929 and consists of a glass or stainless steel cylindrical beaker with a plunger/filter assembly.

Important Settings:

                Coffee-to-Water Ratio: 1:15
                Water Temperature: 90-96°C
                Brew Time: 4 minutes

Step-by-step Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Water: Boil water and let it cool for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Grind the coffee to a coarse consistency.
  3. Warm the French Press.
  4. Add ground coffee to the French Press.
  5. Pour water evenly over the grounds.
  6. Stir gently to ensure all grounds are saturated.
  7. Steep for 4 minutes, then press the plunger down.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your coffee.
Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated brewing method that produces a bold, intense cup of coffee. The signature Espresso Nova blend offers bright acidity, rich chocolate notes, and a smooth finish.

Our Espresso Nova blend is crafted for espresso, with selected Arabica beans that create a well-balanced cup. Perfecting your espresso skills will open a world of coffee possibilities.

Espresso is the foundation of many coffee drinks, such as macchiatos, cappuccinos, and lattes. A good shot of espresso can elevate your home coffee bar to a professional level.

Investing in quality espresso equipment is essential for achieving the best results at home.

Important Settings:

                Dose: 18 grams (Espresso Nova blend)
                Yield: 36 grams (espresso)
                Brew Time: 25-30 seconds
                Water Temperature: 90-95°C
                Pressure: 9 bars

Step-by-step Instructions:

  1. Prepare the espresso machine.
  2. Grind the Espresso Nova blend to a fine consistency.
  3. Dose 18 grams of ground coffee and tamp it firmly.
  4. Brew for 25-30 seconds, observing the flow.
  5. Adjust grind size or tamping pressure if needed.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
